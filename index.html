Hi! We are Group 26.

And this is our project, Analysis of Recent Redtagging of Senatoriables, a deep dive into Tweets that spread misinformation/disinformation regarding senatoriables and their links to terrorist groups. Using Python and data analysis, we aim to determine whether there is a correlation between the redtagging of these senatoriables and the senatorial elections.

Here's an overview of our project.

This project is a time series analysis of mis/disinformation tweets about Redtagging of Senatoriables.

Motivation

The Data Science Team wants to know what event has triggered the spread of mis/disinformation about the redtagging of senatoriables.

Hypothesis

The spread of mis/disinformation about the redtagging of senatoriables is directly influenced by the timing of the elections.

Null Hypothesis

The spread of mis/disinformation about the redtagging of senatoriables is not influenced by the timing of the elections.

Action Plan

Analyze the frequency in relation to the posting date of mis/disinformation tweets about the redtagging of senatoriables.

Contiue reading below to find out our methodology and see our results.
